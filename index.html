Comment Control

Depending on the nature of the ad, comments might have a positive or negative influence on its results. With this feature, you can choose to hide all or the negative comments only.

Dynamic Daily Budget

With this feature, Charlotte will change the AdSet daily budget to optimize it for more results per dollar.

Amazon Attribution

Fast Link

It has been shown that Facebook slows some links down. To avoid having your ads slowed, Charlotte will redirect your customers before landing on the destination link. This makes the deliver faster and cheaper.

Best Age

Different combinations of minimum and maximum age will affect your reach and objective predictions. This feature allows Charlotte to find the best combination possible.

Cloning Ads

The best performing ads, with more and cheaper results, will be cloned at 3am when this feature is used.

CPR Threshold

Automatically pause ads that go over the limit you choose as Cost Per Result threshold, and the time period you want to consider for that limit.

Dynamic Audience

Different Audiences generate different predictions of results. With this feature Charlotte will choose the optimal audience available.

Dynamic Placement

Different combinations of placements will have different predictions on conversions or objectives. With this feature, Charlotte will optimize this selection automatically.

Dynamic Targeting

Choose variations of targets related to your product so Charlotte can rotate them looking for what would generate optimal results. As target effectiveness changes, Charlotte responds by rearranging them accordingly.

Spend Before Results

With this feature you can choose how much you’re willing to spend per ad, before getting any results.

Optimize DMA Regions

Charlotte will select the 10% worst performing DMA regions from your AdSets, and exclude them from your target location. This prevents paying for reach on areas that are not converting well enough.

Revival

Facebook has late attribution. Your ad might receive a result after it has been paused. If with the new attribution your cost per result becomes lower than your CPR threshold, Charlotte will “revive” it, activating the previously paused ad.

ROAS

Choose the minimum ROAS (Return on ad spend) you want for your ads. If it goes under your decided threshold, it is automatically paused.

Objective

Some strategies might involve telling Facebook to optimize your ads for a specific objective, and optimize for something different. In this example, the ads have a Complete Registration as an objective. However, before getting results we could choose to optimize it for Link clicks instead, and change it back to it’s original optimization afterwards.
